Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Learn how to make a simple Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Grape Jelly)

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spread jelly on other slice.
  4. Put two slices together.
